Our Yeshiva

Solidifying identity as bnei Torah

Bais Medrash Ma'alin Bakodesh offers a unique, unparalleled environment where bachurim can solidify their identity as bnei Torah and develop a sense of ownership over their growth in learning and avodas Hashem.

In an atmosphere permeated with genuine warmth and passion for Torah and Yiddishkeit, our approach focuses on taking achrayus for personal growth and discovering the greatness within. Our yeshiva fosters deep personal connections with the rebbeim and a beautiful sense of achdus among the talmidim.

The Program

Designed for the ben aliya

Weekly Retzifus Seder

A structured framework that helps each bachur build consistency and ownership in his learning.

One-on-One with Yungerleit

Personal chavrusahs and ongoing shakla vetarya projects that deepen understanding and connection.

Farbrengens & Outings

Thursday night farbrengens, Motzaei Shabbos outreach, and outings with rebbeim to celebrate accomplishments.
